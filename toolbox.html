    Name-finding engine
    TaxonFinder is a dictionary-based engine. It is more accurate when names are known. NetiNeti is a language processing engine that may discover more names, but can result in false positives.
    Tooltips
    Shortcut Key: Ctrl+Shift+S (Windows) or Command+Shift+S (Mac) to launch NameSpotter on the current tab
    About
    NameSpotter was developed by David P. Shorthouse at the Marine Biological Laboratory for the Global Names project. The name-finding engines, TaxonFinder and NetiNeti were developed by his colleagues.
